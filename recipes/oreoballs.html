Oreo Balls

Description

Oreo Balls are rich, bite-sized treats made by blending crushed Oreos with smooth cream cheese, then coating them in glossy melted baking chocolate. With a soft, fudgy center and a crisp chocolate shell, these no-bake delights are irresistibly indulgent and incredibly easy to make. Perfect for parties, gifting, or satisfying a quick chocolate craving without turning on the oven.

Ingredients

  • Oreos
  • Cream Cheese
  • Baking Chocolate

Steps

  1. Place the Oreos in a bowl and crush them into fine crumbs. You can use a food processor or a rolling pin and some patience.
  2. Add the cream cheese to the crushed Oreos. Mix until everything comes together into a smooth, thick dough with no dry crumbs.
  3. Scoop small portions of the mixture and roll them between your palms to form bite-sized balls.
  4. Place the balls on a plate or tray lined with parchment paper. Refrigerate for about 20â€“30 minutes so they firm up.
  5. Melt the baking chocolate using a double boiler or microwave in short intervals, stirring until smooth and glossy.
  6. Dip each chilled Oreo ball into the melted chocolate, coating it completely. Let excess chocolate drip off.
  7. Place the coated balls back on the lined tray and refrigerate until the chocolate sets fully.
